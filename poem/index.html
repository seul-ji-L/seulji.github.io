Prologue - Yun Dong-ju

Prologue

Yun Dong-ju

I hope to live with a conscience clear

until my dying day

And yet like the windblown leaf

I have suffered

I must love all those close to death

With a heart art sings of the stars.

And take the path

I have been called to walk

Even tonight, the stars are being

Ruffled by the wind.
